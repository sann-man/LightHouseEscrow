Questions

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common escrow questions.

How long does escrow usually take?

Most California escrows close within 30–45 days depending on financing, inspections, and title conditions.

Is my money secure during escrow?

Yes. Funds are held in secure trust accounts and released only when all contractual conditions are satisfied.

What does an escrow company do?

We act as a neutral third party that coordinates documents, timelines, funds, and communication between all parties.

Can buyers choose the escrow company?

Yes. Buyers and sellers can negotiate which escrow company will handle the transaction.
